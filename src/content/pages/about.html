The Neighborhood

Casco Antiguo, the historic heart of Cadiz, beckons with its narrow cobblestone streets, ancient churches, and vibrant plazas. As one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in Europe, its charm lies in its rich tapestry of Moorish, Roman, and Phoenician influences. Wander through labyrinthine alleys lined with colorful facades, stumble upon hidden tapas bars, and soak in the lively atmosphere of bustling markets. From the imposing Cathedral to the picturesque La Caleta beach, Casco Antiguo invites you to immerse yourself in centuries of history and culture amidst its timeless beauty.
